Accomplishments

Selected for Erasmus+ Scholarship
2023

In 2023, I was selected for the prestigious Erasmus+ Scholarship to attend an exchange semester at Universidad Pontifica Comillas de Madrid, Spain. This opportunity allowed me to experience international academic exchange, gain exposure to different educational systems, and learn from global experts in the field of engineering.

FAB Academy 2020 Scholarship
2021

In 2021, I received a 4000 USD scholarship from Kerala Startup Mission and FAB Foundation to attend the FAB Academy 2020 at FAB Lab Kochi, part of MIT's Center for Bits and Atoms. The program covered 3D printing, CNC machining, and electronics design through hands-on fabrication training.

Nomination to World Skills Russia
2020

I was nominated to represent India in World Skills Russia 2020, an international skills competition organized by WorldSkills International. It recognized my abilities in mechanical engineering among a global pool of top talent.

India Skills State Level Winner - Mechanical CAD
2020

I emerged as the Mechanical CAD Category Winner at the India Skills State Level Competition, securing a prize of 1 lac INR (~1400 USD). This competition recognized my technical excellence in CAD and engineering problem-solving.

Finalist in Rural India Business Conclave
2020

I was a finalist in the Rural India Business Conclave (RIBC), where I presented ideas focused on transforming rural livelihoods with technology. I collaborated with entrepreneurs, investors, and policy experts on rural tech innovation models.

IICDC 2019 Semi-Finalist
2019

I reached the semi-finals of the India Innovation Challenge Design Contest (IICDC) organized by DST & Texas Instruments. I presented a product prototype and received mentorship from industry experts to help bring my idea closer to commercialization.
